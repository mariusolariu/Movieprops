Warcraft (alternatively known as Warcraft: The Beginning)[4] is a 2016 American-Chinese action fantasy film directed by Duncan Jones and written by Charles Leavitt and Jones, based on the video game series of the same name. The film stars Travis Fimmel, Paula Patton, Ben Foster, Dominic Cooper, Toby Kebbell, Ben Schnetzer, Robert Kazinsky, Clancy Brown and Daniel Wu. The film portrays the initial encounters between the humans and the orcs and takes place in a variety of locations established in the video game series
